WELCOME!

Naval Dental Clinic, located at #84 Rizal Avenue, Brgy. San Isidro, Taytay, Rizal, is your trusted destination for comprehensive and high-quality dental care. We are dedicated to ensuring the long-term oral health and comfort of patients of all ages—from kids and teens to adults and seniors.

Our wide range of services includes:

  • Preventive Care: Dental Check-Ups and Oral Prophylaxis (cleaning).
  • Restorative Treatments: Tooth Restoration (fillings), Tooth Extraction/Oral Surgery, Endodontics (Root Canal Therapy), and Prosthodontics (Dentures, Crowns, Bridges).
  • Specialized Services: Orthodontic Treatment and Aesthetic & Cosmetic Dentistry.

We combine professional expertise with a focus on patient comfort, making reliable dental care accessible to the Taytay community.
