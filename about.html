About Us

Learn ore about us and what we do at Naval Dental Clinic

The Naval Dental Clinic has served its community for over six decades, offering patient-centered care grounded in family tradition, professional excellence, and continuity across generations. Today, the clinic is managed by Dr. Xavier Naval, her son, who also holds a full-time post at Rizal Medical Center and attends to clinic patients by appointment. The practice now proudly welcomes its third generation, as Dr. Xavier’s daughter—currently a 2nd-year Dentistry student at Centro Escolar University—is preparing to carry on the family legacy.

Dr. Xavier Naval

Doctor of Dental Medicine (DMD) Manila Central University

Dr. Naval is known for his expertise in surgical dental procedures and his commitment to compassionate, evidence-based care. His dual roles in both public hospital service and private practice reflect his dedication to serving a wide range of patients.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, but only if there are available slots. It is still best to book an appointment to secure your schedule.

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash, GCash, Landbank, and BPI. We also offer monthly payments for ongoing procedures, but a 50% downpayment is required to cover laboratory fees and dental materials.

3. How can I book an appointment?

Just send a message through our contact number or email.

4. Do you accept dental insurance or HMO?

For now, we do not accept HMO, but we can provide necessary documents if needed for reimbursement (if applicable).

5. Do you offer free dental check-ups?

Yes, we offer free dental check-ups. Consultation is free, and the pricing for any needed treatment will be discussed after the check-up so you can decide comfortably.

6. Should I bring any documents or X-rays during my visit?

For convenience, we recommend bringing a panoramic X-ray during your check-up. If you don’t have one yet, we will advise you after the consultation so we can properly assess your case and finalize the treatment plan and pricing.
